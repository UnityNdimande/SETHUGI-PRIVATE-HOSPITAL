This is the story of Mma Rakotala, a woman whose dedication to her community in the 1970s earned her the rare honor of having a hospital named after her, a testament to her indomitable spirit and relentless pursuit of betterment for her people.

Her legend grew through the 1970s, a decade marked by her relentless advocacy for better health services in her village. The hospital, inaugurated in the early 1980s, was a testament to Mma Rakotala's hard work and dedication. Today, Sethugi Memorial Hospital is a modern facility, equipped to meet the needs of the population it serves. It provides not only healthcare but also education and training for future generations of healthcare workers, ensuring that Mma Otsile's legacy endures. Mma Rakotala's story is a powerful reminder of the impact one individual can have on the lives of many.
